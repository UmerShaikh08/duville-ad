Riverdale Grand

Welcome to Riverdale Grand, the epitome of luxury living in Kharadi, Pune. Our residential project offers a diverse range of 2, 3, and 4 BHK Flats and Duplexes meticulously designed to redefine your lifestyle. Strategically located amidst the bustling heart of Kharadi, Riverdale Grand boasts proximity to esteemed educational institutions, renowned healthcare facilities, and vibrant shopping hubs, ensuring unparalleled convenience for residents.

Riverdale Grove

Welcome to Riverdale Grove, where urban living harmonizes with nature's tranquillity along the Riverdale Riverfront Road. Our residential community is a serene oasis, drawing inspiration from the lush greenery and woods adorning the riverbank.
